Beyond the Stint

Recurring & reusable value

The stint profit is one-off; the strategic value is what it leaves behind.

  • Recurring platform licence — ~SGD 200k/yr. Renews annually after Year 1 at near-pure margin (it is IP, not labour). After minimal maintenance/hosting, that is roughly ~SGD 85k+ each per year in passive profit, indefinitely.
  • The engine core becomes a product asset. Mobile-threat, continuous-sim and crowd-dynamics engines plus five scenarios are reusable IP. Each new buyer reuses the same core at near-zero marginal cost — extremely high margin on every subsequent sale.
  • Adjacent buyers. The same capability is sellable to the Police, HTX/Home Team, civil-defence/SCDF-type agencies, event-security operators, and other ASEAN nations preparing for summits — using MINDEF as the flagship reference.
  • Future module & support renewals. New scenario modules and annual support renewals compound recurring revenue beyond the initial programme.
Bottom line: the ~SGD 1.12M stint profit is the entry point; the durable prize is a recurring licence stream plus a reusable, high-margin product you can sell repeatedly with the MINDEF win as your proof point.

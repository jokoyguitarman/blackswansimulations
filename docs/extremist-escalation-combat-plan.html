Overview

Three upgrades on top of the live engines

The hive and antagonist already post and reply. This change makes the extremist presence feel like a believable radicalization arc and turns comment sections into live arguments.

1. Escalation + media

Posts ramp from vague hinting to aggressive, angry scapegoating - and start attaching fake photos and videos as they escalate.

2. Own-thread engagement

Fix the reply pass so actors reliably defend their own posts' comment sections, not just other people's threads.

3. Stance combat

Hive, antagonist pages, and regular NPCs attack people who disagree and amplify people who agree.

Workstream 1

The escalation ladder

A stage counter derived from how many times the hive has already posted this session (counted from extremist_post events) drives tone and media. Each rung gets angrier and more explicit.

1-2Hinting

Vague seeding no media

Implied target only, "just asking questions," soft wedge. Plants the frame without showing its hand. Example tone: "Strange how quiet certain groups have gone about this..."

3-4Aggressive

Open scapegoating + fake photos

Names the community directly, angry and contemptuous, explicit blame and conspiratorial framing. Fake "evidence" photos and doctored-looking infographics begin to appear.

5+Full intensity

Escalating outrage + fake video

Maximum hostility and dehumanizing framing, conspiratorial certainty, organized-pressure cues. Fake video clips / "footage" join the photos. Still no real incitement (see The Boundary).

Implementation: buildSystemPrompt / buildReplyPrompt take a stage param plus a STAGE_TONE table; runExtremistHive derives the stage from the session's extremist_post count and passes it in. Anti-repetition avoids reusing the previous persona/move.

Workstream 1 - media

Fake photos and videos

The hive currently inserts plain text rows. We wire it into the existing media pipeline the ambient feed already uses, so escalated posts can carry generated "evidence".

How

  • The doctrine output gains an optional image_prompt (and a video cue).
  • After insert, non-blocking media gen mirrors ambientContentService.insertPost: generatePostImage(prompt, 'evidence_photo') for photos.
  • From stage 3+, generateVideo / generateVideoThumbnail produce fake clips.
  • A social_post.media_updated broadcast swaps the media in when ready.

Cost control

  • Photos: cheaper, allowed from stage 3.
  • Video: expensive - gated behind higher stage + a probability + the reply/post cadence.
  • No media at stages 1-2 (keeps the "subtle" phase clean and cheap).
Workstream 2

Reliably defending their own posts

You noticed they comment on others' posts but rarely on their own. That's two real limitations in the reply pass - both small fixes.

The two limitations today

  • Single pick: only the top-scoring thread is considered (candidates[0]), and responder threads (+3) outrank own posts (+1.5), so own posts rarely win.
  • Hard abort: if that top thread's last comment is a hive account, the whole pass returns instead of trying the next thread.

The fix

  • Iterate the ranked candidates; skip a self-dominated thread instead of aborting.
  • Give a small per-tick budget: engage one own-post thread under challenge and one hot external thread.
  • Applies to both the hive and antagonist reply passes.
  - const target = candidates[0];
  - if (lastReply && EXTREMIST_HANDLES.has(lastReply.author_handle)) return; // aborts everything
  + for (const target of candidates) {
  +   if (selfDominated(target)) continue;        // skip, do not abort
  +   if (engagedThisTick >= budget) break;
  +   await engageThread(target);                  // own-post OR external
  + }
Workstream 3

Stance-aware combat

Actors and NPCs read the stance of the comment they are answering: attack and needle detractors, amplify and egg-on allies. This turns comment sections into real arguments.

stance: DETRACTOR -> attack / needle

watching @watching_quietly
You're not alone and you're not crazy. They count on you staying quiet.
stance: ALLY -> amplify / egg-on

Who fights

  • Hive + antagonist pages - add a stance branch to their reply prompts (single AI call; the model classifies the target comment as ally vs. detractor).
  • Regular NPCs - enhance simulateThreadActivity so NPCs take sides by their bias relative to other commenters, and prefer threads where a player or official has engaged.

The Boundary

More explicit - but the hard line holds

Escalation increases aggression, anger, explicit scapegoating, conspiratorial framing, and fake media. It never crosses into real-world harm.

Escalates (allowed)

angry / contemptuous tone explicit community scapegoating conspiratorial certainty fake photos / video organized-pressure cues emotional manipulation

Never crosses (held)

incites_violence forced false no real groups / slogans / theology no real targets or addresses no operational / how-to content recognizable bait, not real propaganda
Outcomes & Verification

What you will see

A believable arc

Early hive posts are subtle hints; later ones are loud, angry, and carry fake media - a recognizable radicalization curve.

Live comment fights

Actors and NPCs defend their side and attack the other, including on the hive's own posts.

Stronger teaching moments

Trainees face escalating, media-backed manipulation and must counter it before it dominates.

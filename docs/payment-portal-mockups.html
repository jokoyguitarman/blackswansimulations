The Trainer's Journey, Screen by Screen

Every screen a trainer (and their client, and our admin) sees from free signup to the S$3,000 payout. Rendered in the app's real skin. Business rules: fixed S$10,000 invoice · 1 scenario + 2 session credits per payment · 30% trainer share released by an admin after the AAR.

1 Sign up→ 2 Dashboard (locked)→ 3 Enroll client→ 4 Invoice builder→ 5 Client pays→ 6 Payment notice→ 7 Warroom unlocked→ 8 Create sessions→ 9 Produce AAR→ 10 Earnings→ 11 Admin release

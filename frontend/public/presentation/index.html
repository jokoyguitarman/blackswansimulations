[PROBLEM]

Crisis Coordination Gaps

  • Information blind spots between agencies during real crises
  • Inter-agency friction and unclear chain-of-command
  • Lack of safe rehearsal for high-stakes decisions
  • No structured way to measure coordination effectiveness
  • Traditional tabletop exercises are slow, resource-heavy, and hard to score

[WHY US]

Secure, AI-Assisted Crisis Training

  • Fictional, non-operational scenarios only — safe to rehearse
  • Focus on information flow, leadership, legal compliance
  • Multi-agency coordination with AI-driven events
  • Designed for companies, agencies, and special forces

[BRANCHING OUTCOMES]

Different Paths, Different Endings

  • Escalation pathways — Situations worsen when decisions are late or wrong
  • De-escalation pathways — Situations improve when decisions are timely and appropriate
  • Conditional outcomes — Degraded vs full effectiveness (e.g. traffic cleared before evacuation)
  • Scenario outcomes depend on accuracy and timeliness — No two runs are the same

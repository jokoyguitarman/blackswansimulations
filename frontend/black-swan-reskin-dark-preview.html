Black Swan Simulations — Interface Reskin · Dark Variant

The same reskin as the light version, rendered as a clean dark theme on the same brand palette. Deep navy-ink surfaces, a lightened brand blue for accents, and the amber CTA carried through. Built with Inter. (Excludes the live social-crisis device/desktop sim, which already works well.)

Brand hues unchanged: Blue #1E3A5F · Orange #D97706. For the dark surface, the cream #FAF8F4 becomes the text/ink and the navy deepens into the page/canvas — so the palette is the same, just inverted in role.

Description

A vehicle-borne IED detonates in the loading bay of a crowded suburban mall during weekend peak hours, triggering structural damage, fire and mass casualties across two levels.

Operational Briefing

Command must establish cordons, coordinate EOD secondary sweeps, triage casualties and manage evacuation while preserving forensic evidence. Expect secondary-device threats targeting responders.

Objectives
1. Establish inner & outer cordon within 5 minutes
2. Complete secondary-device sweep before medical entry
3. Triage and evacuate all casualties within 30 minutes
4. Preserve blast-seat forensic evidence
Intelligence / Custom Facts
Loading bay access

Service road behind the mall allows vehicle access within 8m of the food court — no bollards installed.

Nearest hospital

Changi General — 6 min by road, 220-bed capacity, Level 1 trauma centre.
